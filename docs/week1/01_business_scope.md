# Week 1 – Business Scope Definition

## Objective
To define a complete enterprise-level eyewear ERP system capable of running full retail operations.

## Core System Areas

1. Inventory & Warehouse Management
2. Procurement & Supplier Control
3. Sales (Online + In-Store)
4. Prescription Handling
5. Finance & Voucher-Based Accounting
6. AI Business Reporting
7. Role-Based Access Control
8. Audit Logging & Security

## Target Outcome

The system must be capable of operating as a full business ERP for SME eyewear retail operations.
