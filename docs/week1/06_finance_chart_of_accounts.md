# Finance – Chart of Accounts (iWear)

## Objective
Provide a standard SME-friendly Chart of Accounts to support voucher-based double-entry accounting.

---

## 1. Assets (1000–1999)

### Current Assets
- 1100 Cash in Hand
- 1110 Cash at Bank
- 1120 Customer Receivables (If credit sales enabled)
- 1130 Inventory Asset (Frames)
- 1140 Inventory Asset (Lenses)
- 1150 Inventory Asset (Accessories)
- 1160 Inventory Adjustment / Variance
- 1170 Input Tax (If tax module used)

### Non-Current Assets
- 1500 Equipment / Fixtures
- 1510 Computer / IT Assets
- 1590 Accumulated Depreciation

---

## 2. Liabilities (2000–2999)

### Current Liabilities
- 2100 Supplier Payables
- 2110 Salaries Payable
- 2120 Utilities Payable
- 2130 Customer Advances (If partial payments used)
- 2140 Output Tax Payable (If tax module used)

---

## 3. Equity (3000–3999)

- 3100 Owner Capital
- 3200 Owner Drawings
- 3300 Retained Earnings

---

## 4. Revenue (4000–4999)

### Sales Revenue
- 4100 Sales – Frames
- 4110 Sales – Lenses
- 4120 Sales – Accessories
- 4130 Sales – Services (Fitting/Repair, etc.)

### Sales Adjustments
- 4200 Sales Discounts
- 4300 Sales Returns (Contra revenue)

---

## 5. Cost of Goods Sold (5000–5999)

- 5100 COGS – Frames
- 5110 COGS – Lenses
- 5120 COGS – Accessories
- 5200 Inventory Loss / Shrinkage

---

## 6. Operating Expenses (6000–6999)

### Staff & Admin
- 6100 Salaries & Wages
- 6110 Staff Allowances
- 6120 Training / Courses

### Utilities
- 6200 Electricity
- 6210 Internet
- 6220 Water / Gas

### Rent & Maintenance
- 6300 Shop Rent
- 6310 Repairs & Maintenance

### Selling & Marketing
- 6400 Advertising / Marketing
- 6410 Delivery Expense

### Office & Misc
- 6500 Office Supplies
- 6510 Software Subscriptions
- 6590 Miscellaneous Expense

---

## Notes
- Account codes can be extended later without breaking design.
- Separate inventory accounts help reporting by category.
